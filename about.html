About

Mathematical Base Defenders is a speed/math game where you can play alone, or with other players (coming soon).

Objective

The objective of Mathematical Base Defenders is to form problems for the enemies by using tiles to match the enemy's "value" before they reach the base.

Play

You form an expression that when evaluated, matches one of the enemies' "requested values." (the number in the middle of the enemy). While you are doing that, the enemies will move towards your base. There are no power-ups or pauses.
