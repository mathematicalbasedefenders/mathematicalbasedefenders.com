About

Mathematical Base Defenders is a speed/math game where you can play alone, or with other players.

Objective

Unlike typical math games, where your job is to solve math problems, the objective of Mathematical Base Defenders is to form problems for the enemies by using tiles to match the enemy's "value" before they reach the base. (i.e. create math problems for the enemies)

Play

You form an expression that when evaluated, matches one of the enemies' "requested values." (the number in the middle of the enemy). While you are doing that, the enemies will move towards your base. There are no power-ups or pauses.

Scoring

You get points when you kill an enemy. You get more points if the problem's "length" is long enough, the enemy's position is far from the base, and you have killed multiple enemies in succession. (combo)

Problem Length Score Multiplier
6 or less ×1
7 to 13 ×1+0.1(length-6)
14 or more ×1+0.2(length-9)

Enemy's Position Score Multiplier
Less than 50% from base ×1
50% or more from base ×1+0.1(sPosition-5)

Combo Score Multiplier
0 ×1
1 or more ×1.1combo

Base

Your base has 10 health points. If an enemy gets into your base, you will lose 1 health point. Lose all health points and the game is over.
