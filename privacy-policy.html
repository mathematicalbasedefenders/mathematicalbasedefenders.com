Privacy Policy17 July 2021


0. Introduction
Mathematical Base Defenders is an online “educational” math game designed for people of all ages. Mathematical Base Defenders can improve your mental math skills, your brain, and your mouse aim. But in order for us to do that, The service has to collect and store some of your data. However, we will try to be as transparent on our data collection processes as possible, and this privacy policy may help fulfill that goal.
1. Data Mathematical Base Defenders Collects
When you sign up for an account the service will collect the following information:
   Username
   E-mail address
   Password (salted and hashed)
Aside from this, other data may be collected because of where we host our services.
Except for the username, none of this information will be shown publicly.
2. Disclosures Of Your Data The service may disclose data to third parties for the following reasons:
   Analyzing website traffic
The third parties are obligated not to disclose that information for other reasons.
3. Your Rights
The service allows you to change your:
   Username
   Email
   Password (salted and hashed after change)
4. Security
Mathematical Base Defenders securely stores your data in MongoDB.
5. Children
Mathematical Base Defenders is designed for all ages. However, if you are under 13, before signing up for our service, please make sure you have permission from a parent or a guardian (teacher, etc.) If we find a user who is under 13 years of age signed up for the service without permission, we will try our best to delete that information from our databases.
6. Other Sites
Our privacy policy only applies to this site. If you visit a different site, you should read their privacy policy.
7.Changes To This Privacy Policy
If we decide to update our Privacy Policy, an announcement will be made on our website.
